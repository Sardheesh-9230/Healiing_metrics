Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my health data?

Your health data is encrypted using industry-leading standards. We comply with HIPAA regulations and implement multiple layers of security to ensure your information remains private and secure at all times.

Can I export my health data?

Yes, Healing Metrics allows you to export your health data in various formats including PDF, CSV, and JSON. You can easily share your information with healthcare providers or download it for personal records.

How often is the dashboard updated?

Our dashboard updates in real-time as new data is received from your connected devices. Historical data is processed and analyzed daily to provide you with the most accurate trends and insights.
